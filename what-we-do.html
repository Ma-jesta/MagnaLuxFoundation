Our Operations

Equipping a
Light-Bearing Generation.

Through intentional discipleship and capacity building, we root young people in God's Word and empower them with the skills to lead and transform their worlds.

Programs & Activities

Weekly Gatherings

School-based devotional and worship sessions centered on Scripture to deepen Word engagement.

Biblical Seminars

Gospel concerts and seminars designed to ignite a burning passion for Christ in schools.

Spiritual Camps

Monthly retreats and camps for immersive spiritual growth and prayerful fellowship.

Practical Equipping

Empowering youth with vocational and digital skills to thrive in life and ministry.

Kingdom Mentorship

Career guidance that integrates faith and profession, offering godly counsel to students.

Soul-Winning

Evangelism outreaches to reach schools and communities with the light of God's Word.
